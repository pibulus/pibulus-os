A radio station for people who like music more than their taste in music.
WHAT IS THIS?
A pirate radio station running on a Raspberry Pi 5 in a house in Brunswick, Melbourne. 24/7 music with a taste level but no format. No ads. No tracking. No subscriptions. Just a hard drive doing its best.
WHAT KIND OF MUSIC?
All of it. Punk, jazz, salsa, metal, ambient, hip-hop, Afrobeat, hyperpop, country, dub, soul, whatever. The curation guarantees a floor — nothing evil, nothing obvious — but within that, anything can happen. The fun is in not knowing what's next.
WHY DOES THIS EXIST?
Because Spotify's algorithms are a con. Because radio compartmentalises people into taste categories. Because every dollar or minute taken away from platforms that exploit musicians is worth it. We're bigger than genre. It's time to build our own platforms and think local digitally.
HOW SHOULD I LISTEN?
Hit play and let it ride. 10,000+ tracks across every genre and era. Not recognising half the songs is a feature. Give it half an hour — it gets addictive. Search and request from the full catalog, no account needed. (Want a radio show? Hit me up.)
WHAT'S COMING?
Thumbs up / thumbs down on tracks — the more people vote, the better the station gets for everyone. Emoji reactions. Shareable links to what's playing. Guest radio shows. Station IDs. More music. Whatever — it's pirate radio. The frequency must grow.
D.I.T. — DOING IT TOGETHER
Got a weird playlist? A radio show idea? A 3am ambient set? Send it through. The whole point is that this thing gets shaped by the people listening to it. The more people contribute, the weirder and better it gets.
WILL IT GO DOWN?
Almost certainly. It's an 0 raspberry pi strapped to a hard drive on a desk in my house. It crashes when I look at it funny. But it always comes back. Uptime is a corporate metric. We measure in vibes.
WHO MADE THIS?
Pablo. Medium sized Mexican-Australian in Brunswick, Melbourne. Card carrying Subgenius. Plays in Mesa Cosa, runs Bone Soup touring, codes at GitHub. Part of the Quick Cat Club. Pro choice, pro Palestine, pro wrestling.
